# Circular: NAAC Peer Team Visit
From the Dean's Office · 1 July 2026

The NAAC peer team visits the Yelahanka campus from Tuesday 14 July to
Thursday 16 July 2026.

What schools must do:
- Documentation folders (course files, attendance registers, jury records)
  updated and handed to the admin office by Friday 10 July 2026, 4 pm.

  meera.admin@srishti.ac.in by Wednesday 8 July.
- Studios remain in normal working state during the visit — the team walks
  through live classes; no staged presentations.

Contact for NAAC queries: Prof. Lakshmi Prasad (Design-led Innovation),
coordinator for accreditation, available Tue–Thu 10 am to 1 pm, N-block 204.
